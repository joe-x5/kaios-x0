LED Banner (v. 1.0.0)
by Valeriy Skachko
valesk.com

LED Banner is an amazing new app that makes creating LED scrollers a breeze! With this app, you have the power to create stunning text LED boards with the touch of a button. With unique and cool LED banner effects your scrolling messages will be unlike any others.

The app includes a user-friendly and intuitive interface that makes creating LED banners and scrolling texts easy and fun. With a few simple clicks, you can change the font size, color, and scroll direction of your text LED board.

Whether you are at a concert, welcoming a celebrity, attending a music festival, watching a sports game, participating in an e-sports event, or just expressing yourself and interacting with others, simply lift up this LED board and let your heart shine brightly. You will definitely be the highlight of the crowd.
